Frequently Asked Questions

How does Claro work?

Claro uses advanced AI to translate conversations in real-time. Simply speak or type in your language, and Claro will translate it to the desired language instantly.

Which languages are supported?

Claro currently supports over 50 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Chinese, Japanese, and many more. We're constantly adding new languages.

Do I need an internet connection to use Claro?

Yes, Claro requires an internet connection to access our AI translation servers for the most accurate translations.

How accurate are the translations?

Claro's translations are highly accurate for everyday conversations. Our AI is trained on billions of conversations to provide natural-sounding translations.

Can I use Claro offline?

Currently, Claro requires an internet connection for full functionality. We're working on an offline mode for basic translations in the future.
